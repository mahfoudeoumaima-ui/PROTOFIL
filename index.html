About Me

A mind that lives between code and art

I'm Oumaima — a student at Solicode with a deep passion for the intersection of design and engineering. I don't just build interfaces; I craft experiences that feel intentional, elegant, and alive.

My toolkit spans both worlds: front-end development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP, paired with UI/UX design in Figma — wireframing, prototyping, and user-centered thinking. I believe the best digital products are born when logic and aesthetics coexist.

Quiet by nature, I do my deepest thinking in the space between inspiration and execution. Every project I touch becomes a story.
